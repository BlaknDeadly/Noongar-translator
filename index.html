About Noongar Language:
Noongar is the language of the Noongar people of southwestern Australia. It has 14 distinct dialects spoken across different regions of Western Australia. This translator provides authentic words from Rose Whitehurst's Official Noongar Dictionary (1992), George Fletcher Moore's 1842 Descriptive Vocabulary, and AIATSIS archival records.
